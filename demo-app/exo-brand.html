01 — Brand Identity

Not a wallet.
A companion.

exo is an agentic wallet built on Glove. It doesn't just hold your assets — it watches, reacts, learns, and plays. Where Solflare channels medieval authority, exo channels living energy. It's your curious, playful financial sidekick on Solana.

Positioning

The first wallet that feels alive. An agentic, self-custody companion for Solana — powered by Glove.

Tagline

"your wallet has opinions."

Archetype

The Trickster / The Companion — curious, energetic, loyal. Watches what you're doing and reacts with personality.

02 — Iconography

Theme Modes

Dark Mode

The default canvas. Deep void backgrounds let the lime accent punch through. Every interaction feels electric.

Warm paper tones. The lime shifts slightly darker for contrast. Feels like writing on a notepad with a highlighter.

05 — Voice & Tone

Speaks like a friend
who knows crypto.

Where Solflare channels medieval authority ("your sword and shield"), exo channels living energy. It's witty, warm, slightly chaotic, and always paying attention.

Playful

Never corporate. Always conversational. Personality in every pixel.

"nice swap. that was kinda elegant actually."

Curious

Interested in what you're doing. Asks questions. Notices patterns.

"you've been checking SOL/USDC a lot today. want me to set an alert?"

Confident

Knows its stuff. Gives clear, opinionated guidance when asked.

"gas is spiking. i'd wait 10 minutes if this isn't urgent."

Celebratory

Gets excited when things go well. Makes wins feel earned.

"LET'S GO. +23% this week. you deserve that."

Protective

Watches out for you without being paranoid or preachy.

"heads up — this contract looks sketchy. want me to dig deeper?"

Lowercase

Default voice is casual lowercase. ALL CAPS only for celebrating or emphasis.

"exo speaks in lowercase. it's chill like that."

06 — The Mascot

Meet exo.

A lime square creature with curious eyes. It follows your cursor, reacts to your actions, and always has something to say. It's the living embodiment of the wallet's personality.

hmm, what are they looking at? 👀

👀 Follows your cursor

The mascot's eyes track your mouse position within any container. It's always watching — in a friendly way.

🫧 Reacts to actions

Jumps on successful transactions. Squishes on taps. Shakes on errors. Spins on loading. Every state has a reaction.

💬 Has opinions

Thought bubbles appear contextually. Comments on gas prices, token movements, idle time. Never annoying, always optional.

😴 Gets tired

After idle time, it slowly blinks, then dozes off. Tap to wake it up — it startles adorably.

🎨 Adapts to theme

Lime in dark mode, dark in light mode. Can wear tiny accessories for achievements or seasons.

08 — Sound Design

Sounds alive.

Every interaction has an optional sound. Short, synthesized, playful — never annoying. Inspired by game UI sounds. Users can toggle sounds off, but the defaults make exo feel physical.

09 — UI Components

Playful primitives.

Pill buttons, generous radius, bouncy hover states. Every component should feel tappable and responsive — like a toy that rewards interaction.
